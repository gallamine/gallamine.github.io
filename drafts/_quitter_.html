"_Quitter_"

Tweet

By William Cox

In misc.

I've recently starting reading the book Quitter by Jon Acuff. It was recommended first by John D. Cook on Twitter:

If you're thinking about quitting your job, check out Quitter by Jon Acuff. @johndcook

and then, strangely enough, in a random email from my mother:

"Maybe one of you should buy this book and pass it around. Quitter by Jon Acuff"

"Ok," I thought, "two recomendations in two days. I guess I should spend the money." I'm enjoying it so far. Seems like sound advice. I'm terrible at book reviews, but I did find this quote revealing:

... if finding your dream is an act of recovery, that changes everything.

You don't ask the bottomless, "What do I want to do with my life?" but instead, "What have I done in my life that I loved doing?" Instead of a million different options from out there, you're suddenly left with a manageable handful of options from within your own experience.
